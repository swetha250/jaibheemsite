Founder of Jaibheem Bharath Party

Gou. Sri. Jada Sravan Kumar Former Civil Judge and President of Mundadugu Party.



About JaiBheem Bharath Party?

Founded on 14-April-2022, the JaiBheem Bharath Party is dedicated to achieving the aims and objectives of the Baba Saheb Dr. B.R. Ambedkar ideology. The party represents the interests of Bahujan's in India. In order to achieve political power and human integrity, you must maintain your self-respect, equal rights, and self-respect.

Detailing about JBP Founder and Inspiration?

Taking into consideration a conservative made by Mundadugu Party members and making a representation in the general body, it was unanimously decided to change the name of the party to Jaibheem Bharath Party. 

Early Stage (Born & Brought Up), Student Life, Career, Achievements, path to JBP Foundation?

Sri. Jada Sravan Kumar was born and raised in Guntur, spent his student life in different parts of Guntur District, and was actively involved in student organizations by the name of AISF. Schooling at PrathiPadu High School and pre-university courses at Andhra Christian College, Guntur. Chirala is where he finished his graduation and postgraduate studies. From Andhra University, Visakhapatnam, he graduated with a law degree. His professional career started with a renowned insurance company as a legal advisor. Selected for the 1st Class Magistrate status by the Andhra Pradesh Judiciary and worked in several districts across the state. (East Godavari, Ongole and Prakasam Districts). He is the only person who resigned from the Civil Judge Services at a young age to serve the people. As well as that, he began practicing as an Andhra Pradesh High Advocate.
